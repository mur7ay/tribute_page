STEVE JOBS

A Pioneer of the Personal Computer

Timeline of Steve Job's Life:

  • 1976 - Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak co-found Apple.
  • 1984 - Presents the 128K Macintosh Computer.
  • 1985 - Fired from Apple.
  • 1986 - Founded Pixar.
  • 1997 - Returns to Apple.
  • 2001 - Presents the iPod.
  • 2003 - Launches iTunes.
  • 2004 - Undergoes surgery to remove a cancerous tumor from his pancreas.
  • 2007 - Launches the iPhone.
  • 2009 - Takes a six month break to undergo a liver transplant.
  • 2010 - Launches the iPad.
  • Aug. 24th 2011 - Jobs resigns as Apple's CEO.
  • Oct. 5th 2011 - Jobs passes away due to respiratory arrest which was a complication of his cancer.
